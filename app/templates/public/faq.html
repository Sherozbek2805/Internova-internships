Frequently Asked Questions

What is Internova? ⌄

Internova is a platform connecting students with internships and helping companies find young talent efficiently.

Who can use Internova? ⌄

Students, graduates, and companies looking for interns can all use Internova.

Is Internova free? ⌄

Yes — students can use it completely free. Companies may have premium features later.

How do I apply for internships? ⌄

Create an account, browse listings, and apply with a single click.

Can I track my applications? ⌄

Yes — your dashboard shows status updates like reviewing, shortlisted, or accepted.

How do companies select candidates? ⌄

Companies can filter candidates, review profiles, and shortlist the best matches.

Is my data secure? ⌄

Absolutely. We prioritize user privacy and use secure systems to protect your data.

Can I post internships as a company? ⌄

Yes — companies can create listings and manage applicants easily.
